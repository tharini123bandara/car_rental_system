Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What documents do I need to rent a car?

A valid, original driving license should be presented by the vehicle renter and/or the driver when picking up the vehicle. A driving experience for a certain time period is not necessary. Customers who do not own a Sri Lankan driver's license should present a valid government issued ID card or passport, along with an international driver's permit.
